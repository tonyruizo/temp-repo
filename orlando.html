Explore Orlando

Orlando is known for its theme parks, such as Walt Disney World Resort, Universal Studios, and SeaWorld. Every year, millions of visitors come to Orlando, making it the entertainment capital of Florida. The city has a lively downtown area, many golf courses, and great shopping spots.
